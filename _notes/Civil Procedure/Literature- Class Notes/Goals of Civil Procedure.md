# Goals of Civil Procedure
Created: 2022-08-27 23:24

**Primary Goals of Dispute Resolution:
	- Accuracy
	- Efficiency
	- Finality
	- Neutrality
	- Equality
	- Fairness
	- Accessibility
	- Consistency
	- Reduce Bias & Prejudice
	- Transparency
