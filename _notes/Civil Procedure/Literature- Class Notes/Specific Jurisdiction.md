Jurisdiction is establish when:

• *International Shoe* Rule- minimum contacts w/forum state  
• Focus on D’s contacts with forum state  
• D’s Purposeful Availment of the benefits & protections of the forum  
state  
• D’s reasonable anticipation that they might be sued for the claims in  
that state  
• Relatedness – Claims must arise out of or relate to D’s contacts with state  
AND substantial justice & fair play
