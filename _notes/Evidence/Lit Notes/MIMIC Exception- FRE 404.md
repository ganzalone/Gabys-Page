Evidence of a crime, wrong, or act...may be admissible for another purpose, such as proving:  
• Motive  
• Intent  
• Absence of Mistake  
• Identity  
• Common Scheme or Plan  
• Opportunity  
• Preparation  
• Knowledge  
• Lack of Accident


  
- Can be a bad “act” not only a crime
- “Bad act” may occur after the crime charged
- 404(b) applies to both civil and criminal cases (but mostly used in criminal)
- 404(b) evidence is presented as testimony about the crime/bad act
